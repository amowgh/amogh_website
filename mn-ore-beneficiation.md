Project under Dr. Ravindra Taiwade, Department of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering, VNIT Nagpur (September – November 2023).

Beneficiated manganese ore through a combination of **hydrocyclone separation, roll crushing, and planetary ball milling**, working to optimize ore quality and yield across the different processing stages. An early, hands-on introduction to mineral processing and beneficiation — separate from the additive-manufacturing-and-mechanical-testing thread that runs through most of my later work, but the same underlying instinct: process parameters determine material quality as much as the feedstock does.
