Research project under Dr. S.R. Reddy, Department of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering, VNIT Nagpur (May – July 2025).

A research paper on how **layer thickness and infill pattern** affect the mechanical properties of FDM-printed PLA specimens. Mechanical characterization combined tensile testing and three-point bend testing, monitored with **Digital Image Correlation (DIC)** for full-field strain data rather than a single extensometer trace. Post-fracture, specimens were examined under **SEM** to connect the mechanical response back to the actual failure mechanism — porosity between layers, interlayer bond quality, and how a crack actually propagates through a printed part, rather than treating it as a bulk isotropic material.
