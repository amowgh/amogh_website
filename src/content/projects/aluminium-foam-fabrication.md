Project under Prof. Atul Ballal, Department of Metallurgical & Materials Engineering, VNIT Nagpur (July 2024 – April 2025).

A multi-stage process to synthesize aluminium foam using **recycled aluminium from cans** and **eggshell powder as the foaming agent** — a genuinely sustainable substitution on both the metal and the foaming-agent side. Trials varied the foaming agent's volume fraction to optimize foam structure, with **SEM** used throughout for microstructural analysis to evaluate foam quality and performance.
